This data is from the 3rd census of the ForestGEO (Global Earth Observatory) plot at the Smithsonian Conservation Biology Institute in Front Royal, VA. The data was collected in Summer 2018, and is part of a series of 5-year censuses and is openly available on GitHub. The goal of this dashboard is to allow users to explore the kind of data collected, here represented by a map of all Liriodendron tulipifera trees, along with DBH (diameter at breast height) and whether the trees were recorded as alive or dead. The topmost panel shows a rough aggregation of the species by genera, showing the distribution for this third census.
