Billy

Billy was an elephant who came to the Lope village. Everyone knew him, and he was very gentle around people. He was found as an orphaned baby because of poachers, and the staff at the Research station further in to the National Park saved and raised him until he was old enough to go on his own. Although he did go out on his own, he would randomly come back and visit the station, just hanging around for a few days. Unlike other elephants, Billy was pretty much alone for most of his life.
